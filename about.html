More About the TTI

The Troubled Teen Industry claims to fix any problem a teen has and they directly market to parents with troubled teens or teens who need to be “fixed”. In these terrible facilities, teens are given no rights and almost most of the time consent is not asked for. In these camps teens are put through inhumane ways to “fix” them such as food and sleep deprivation, labor, physical abuse, and sexual abuse. Boot camps use military tactics to get rid of drug use or psychological issues within the teen. The participants receive almost or no water and food along with emotional abuse.
